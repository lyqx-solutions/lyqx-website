GIDY - B2B Delivery Platform for Outdoor Power Equipment

GIDY was built to streamline how contractors, fleet managers, and service providers interact with authorized OPE dealers. The platform combines fast parts ordering, real-time service scheduling, and direct delivery trackingall in one tool designed for job-site speed and dealer-side control.

We designed the system from scratch, mapping role-specific workflows and building mobile-first interfaces that prioritize clarity and task execution. From UX to backend, every layer was crafted to support fast decisions, accurate fulfillment, and reliable communication between all parties.
